The preterite, future, and conditional exhibit SM in questions and negatives (in writing - in speech, often in statements as well; and both particularly for the future and conditional.) The preterite in particular is rarely used in speech, periphrastic constructions with gwneud or ddaru being predominant.

1.1.1.1 Periphrastic tenses

  • Present: present tense of bod + yn + VN
  • Perfect: present tense of bod + wedi + VN
  • Imperfect: imperfect of bod + yn + VN
  • Pluperfect: imperfect of bod + wedi + VN
  • Future: future of bod + yn + VN
  • Future perfect: future of bod + wedi + VN
  • Conditional: conditional of bod + yn + VN (nb: used for closed conditionals; open conditionals are usually os + future, present, perfect, or future perfect)
  • Conditional perfect: conditional of bod + wedi + VN
  • Perfect continuous: …wedi bod yn…
